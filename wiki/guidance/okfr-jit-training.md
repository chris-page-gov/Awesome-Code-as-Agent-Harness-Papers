# OKFR JIT Training

This page is the canonical home for short, content-specific reader training.
The full version will be generated during the OKFR rollout with committed
screenshots under `wiki/assets/okfr-jit/`.

The training will cover:

- Entering through the hamburger list, timeline, and search.
- Reading node colors, direction arrows, relationship chips, and side-panel
  references.
- Moving from overview to focused evidence.
- Choosing graph, timeline, tile, or stack-of-cards views.
- Recognizing inferred relationships and source-review boundaries.
